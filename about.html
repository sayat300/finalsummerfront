Welcome to Sayat's Pizza

In the heart of Astana, 2023 marked the dawn of a culinary revolution as Sayat's Pizza emerged onto the vibrant food scene. Born from a vision to redefine fast food, our founders aimed to blend the rich traditions of Kazakhstan with global flavors. Sayat's Pizza quickly became a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a fusion of authenticity and innovation. From the very beginning, our chefs curated a menu that harmonized local ingredients with international inspirations, offering a symphony of tastes that delighted every palate.
